Current focus

Selected student projects

Hands-on work from campus labs, club initiatives, and hackathons where I focused on building something tangible.

Capstone concept

Table-top Pick-and-Place Arm

Designed a 4 DOF arm with 3D-printed links, MG996R servos, and an STM32 controller. Iterated on joint tolerances and PID values to improve repeatability for light payloads.

  • Modelled the arm in Fusion 360 and validated clearances
  • Implemented inverse kinematics in C with safety limits
  • Documented build steps for club members to replicate
Hackathon build

Smart Campus Delivery Bot

Collaborated with a 4-person team to prototype an indoor robot that maps hallways and carries lab equipment between rooms.

  • Integrated LiDAR data with SLAM on a Jetson Nano
  • Designed a modular payload bay for different equipment
  • Presented testing results to judges and faculty mentors
Club initiative

Assistive Lab Sensor Network

Built a network of low-cost sensor nodes that track humidity and vibration across the robotics lab to catch issues before build sessions.

  • Deployed ESP32 modules with MQTT messaging
  • Wrote a lightweight dashboard for the club workshop leads
  • Created troubleshooting guides for new members
