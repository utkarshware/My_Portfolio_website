Capstone concept

Table-top Pick-and-Place Arm

A 4 DOF manipulator using 3D-printed links and MG996R servos tuned through iterative PID experiments. Firmware on STM32 and inverse kinematics calibration with MATLAB notebooks.

  • Laser-cut base reduces vibration by 28% compared to v1
  • Added soft gripper inserts for lab asset handling
  • Published build guide for Robotics Club onboarding
Hackathon build

Smart Campus Delivery Bot

Built during a 36-hour sprint. Integrated LiDAR-based SLAM on Jetson Nano, modular payload bay, and fall-back teleop for safety demos with faculty.

  • Mapped three academic blocks with sub-15cm accuracy
  • Routed ROS topics over Wi-Fi for debugging from dorms
  • Won "Best Practical Prototype" at InnovateX 2024
Club initiative

Assistive Lab Sensor Network
